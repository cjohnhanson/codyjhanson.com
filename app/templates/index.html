My name is Cody.

I work as a Systems Engineer at PEAK6 Investments in Chicago.

Prior to joining PEAK6 I attended the University of Chicago as an Odyssey Scholar. While in college, I worked as a tutor in the Computer Science Instructional Laboratories where I led a small team of students who managed all of the labs' operations. While studying at the University of Chicago, I also worked as part of the teaching staff for introductory level computer science department courses. For a class called "Introduction to Programming for the World Wide Web", I helped teach basic HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PHP. For another class called "Multimedia Programming as an Interdisciplinary Art", I helped to teach fundamental computer science concepts, including algorithms and data structures using Livecode. I received my BS in computer science and BA in linguistics in June 2017.

During the summer of 2016, I worked as a Digital Tools and Solutions Intern for Procter and Gamble in Cincinnati. Over the course of the summer, I worked with non-technical employees and developed custom business tools in Python to automate many of their most common tasks such as security policy compliance auditing, analysis of spending, and processing invoices from vendors. I also worked with P&G's nascent data science group to develop a tool to process big geospatial datasets using PySpark and Hive.

I have an abiding interest in DevOps and leverage DevOps tools and practices in both professional and personal projects. Some of the tools I like to work with include:

  • Saltstack and Ansible for orchestration and configuration management
  • VSphere and Proxmox for virtualization
  • Bash for scripting and automation
  • Python for ETL, scripting, automation, monitoring, and reporting
  • Nagios, Check_MK, and Foreman for monitoring
  • Django and Flask for web development
My go-to language for large-scale projects as well as quick scripting and automation is Python, but in personal, educational, and professional projects I've also written code in (in decreasing order of proficiency):
  • Bash (with sed and awk)
  • Go
  • Javascript (as well CSS and HTML)
  • PHP
  • C
  • SML
  • Elm
I've also worked with AWS and the Hadoop ecosystem and have familiarity with PostgreSQL (along with psycopg2) and MongoDB (along with PyMongo ).

In my free time I like to read, cook, and listen to and play music. I have a particular interest in longform journalism and narrative nonfiction. I bake bread (well), play guitar and piano (poorly), and collect vinyl.
